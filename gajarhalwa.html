Gajar Halwa

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg carrots, grated
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 200 g sugar
  • 100 g ghee
  • 10-12 cashew nuts
  • 10-12 almonds
  • 5-6 cardamom pods, powdered
  • Raisins for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add grated carrots. Saut√© for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add milk and cook on low flame until the milk evaporates, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. In a separate pan, fry cashew nuts and almonds in ghee until golden brown.
  5. Mix the fried nuts into the halwa and garnish with raisins.
  6. Serve hot or cold.
