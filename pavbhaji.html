Pav bhaji

Ingredients:


  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 pav (bread rolls)
  • Butter for toasting

Instructions:


  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add capsicum and green peas, cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add mashed potatoes, pav bhaji masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cook on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Toast the pav with butter on a griddle.
  7. Serve hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.
