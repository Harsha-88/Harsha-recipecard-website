Veg Biryani

Ingredients:


  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, tomato, peas, potatoes, beans)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • Fried onions and cashews for garnish

Instructions:


  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  6. In a large pot, layer half of the cooked rice. Add the vegetable mixture on top.
  7. Add the remaining rice. Sprinkle chopped coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk (if using).
  8. Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes
